Powerful Community Features

Everything you need to stay connected, informed, and engaged

Safety Alerts

Receive real-time notifications about important safety concerns, weather alerts, and emergency information in your neighborhood.

Issue Reporting

Easily report community issues like broken streetlights, potholes, or graffiti directly through the app for faster resolution.

Report an Issue

Neighborhood Groups

Connect with neighbors who share your interests, hobbies, and community goals. Join existing groups or create your own.

Explore Groups

Community Events

Discover and organize local events, from block parties to neighborhood clean-ups, to strengthen community bonds.

View Events

Interactive Map

Visualize community information on an interactive map showing reported issues, upcoming events, and local resources.

Local Discussions

Engage in civil conversations about neighborhood topics, share recommendations, and get advice from those who know your area best.

Resource Sharing

Share tools, skills, and resources with neighbors through our community marketplace, reducing waste and building relationships.

Volunteer Coordination

Find volunteer opportunities or request help from neighbors for community initiatives and personal assistance.

Local Business Directory

Discover and support neighborhood businesses with our curated directory of local shops, services, and restaurants.

Verified Neighbors

Feel secure knowing that community members are verified residents, creating a trusted network you can count on.

Community Announcements

Stay informed with important updates from local officials, HOA boards, and community leaders in one centralized place.

Safety Analytics

Access neighborhood safety trends and statistics to better understand and address community concerns.
